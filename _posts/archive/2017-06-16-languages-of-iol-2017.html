Registrations are closed and the local organisers, problem committee, jury, board, team leaders and participants from all the countries are preparing for this years event in Dublin!

This year the international linguistics olympiad will be held in 22 languages. These are:
  • Armenian - Armenia
  • Bangla - Bangladesh
  • Bulgarian - Bulgaria 
  • Chinese - China & Taiwan
  • Czech - Czech republic
  • Dutch - Netherlands 
  • English - UK, USA, India, Australia, Canada, Ireland & Isle of Man
  • Estonian - Estonia
  • French - Canada 
  • Hungarian - Hungary
  • Japanese - Japan
  • Korean - Republic of Korea
  • Latvian - Latvia
  • Polish - Poland
  • Portuguese (Brazilian) - Brazil
  • Romanian - Romania 
  • Russian - Russia & Kazakstan 
  • Slovene - Slovenia
  • Spanish - Spain
  • Swedish - Sweden
  • Turkish - Turkey
  • Ukrainian - Ukraine
That's 4 more than last year! Korean, Armenian, Latvian and French.
