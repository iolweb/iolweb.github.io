The results from IOL 2016 are up! You can view them all here.

As usual, the IOL-jury corrects the solutions submitted by the participants in several different languages. Participants can compete in any language of a national contest, though only 1 and teams need to pick only 1 language. The jury is divided up by problems, not languages, meaning that the jury members correcting a certain problems does so in all languages of the contest. This is an impressive feat, and as usual we'd like to acknowledge this and congratulate the jury on yet another successful contest!
