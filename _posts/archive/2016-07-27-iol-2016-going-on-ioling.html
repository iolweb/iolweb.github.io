The 14th International Linguistics Olympiad is currently underway in Mysore, India. The individual contest has taken place, the team contest is soon under way! All the best wishes to all participants, and strength and endurance to the jury during their long hard work correcting. Remember, they correct solutions for the problems written in all languages of the IOL-teams! (For more on that, go here.)

For posts from those present in Mysore, check out the #ioling tags on Instagram, Facebook and Twitter!
