The International Linguistics Olympiad needs a symbol - a flag. We hereby open up a comest for what it should be. Submissions are welcome until the 15th of July and the winner will be selected by the board of the IOL.

Important information to know:

  • the IOL is an international event with participants from all over the world
  • the symbol/flag should be easily recognisable
  • it should


- easily remembered and recognized - after seen once or twice
- not too complex - in order to be recognized and to be possible to be printed in small sizes, so not too much details in it
- available in both full colour and black and white (greyscale) versions without losing the idea
- the meaning of used symbols (if any) to be “obvious” or understood easily by most people form all countries (like Mercedes star)

Optional:
- a specific shape and contour, again easy the remember and recognize (Coca Cola both the name and the bottle)
- may include the name or abbreviation in a specific (own) font, again possible to be read by everyone
- may include simple symbols of one or all meaning of the three words - international (globe is the easiest possible), linguistics (i can’t give an example here) and olympiad (are the circles usable in science olympiads too?)
